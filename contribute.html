// The Pull-Request Pipeline

How a Contribution Flows

Same energy as the episode pipeline: every change runs the gates before it hits main. Public face is effortless; private practice is airtight.

step 01

🍴 Fork & branch

Branch off using the house style: bRaiNRoTRePoRt666X/<topic>-patch-1. One focused change per branch.

step 02

🛠️ Make the change

Follow the naming conventions below. Never overwrite assets — always bump the -vN version.

step 03

⚖️ Run the gates

Sanity-check ethics with ./scripts/reports/campaign-ethics.sh and keep episode metadata in sync with metadata/episodes/template.json.

step 04

🔀 Open a PR

Target main. Describe the what + the why. Link any related issue or episode ID.

step 05

👀 Review

A maintainer reviews against the secretly-ethical checklist. Address feedback on the same branch.

step 06

✅ Merged

It lands on main and ships to Pages. You did a based thing. Now go touch grass. 🌱

// The Based Backbone

Code of Conduct

The whole bit is that it's actually real. Contributors hold the same line as the content.

Be excellent to each other. We're loud and unhinged in public and genuinely kind in private. By contributing you agree to keep the campaign secretly ethical:

  • ❌ No harassment, hate, or targeted attacks — in issues, PRs, or content
  • ❌ No misinformation; cite sources for any factual claim
  • ❌ No undisclosed paid promotion or stolen/unlicensed assets
  • ❌ No exploitation for clout — community benefit comes first

Read the full philosophy in the Ethics Manifesto and docs/ethics/campaign-principles.md.
